Xinghao Zhu

Hi there! I'm a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in control and learning at the University of California, Berkeley. I have the privilege of working with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka, a distinguished member of National Academy of Engineering. Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor's degree from the Honors Youth Program at Xi'an Jiaotong University. My research focuses on the intersection of robotics, planning, and embodied AI.
